About

Island Togs derives from These local clothing with authentic custom designs that showcase the island's rich history and culture.

The name Togs comes from the informal word togs for 'clothing', which itself was probably derived from the word toga, a Roman garment. We came up with these brands and designs equally inspired by life's simple pleasures with clothes for any occassions, mood and from anywhere you will be. We put together our men's and women's collections each season that are focused on sustainability, environmental conservation, and giving back. No matter where your next adventure takes you, we're here to help you realize your dream of "island living" with casual, authentic custom designs that lets you dress like you're on vacation!
